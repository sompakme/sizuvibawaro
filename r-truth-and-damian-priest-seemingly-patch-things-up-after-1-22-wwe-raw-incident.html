R-Truth and Damian Priest Seemingly Patch Things Up After 1/22 WWE RAW Incident

July 04, 2024
blog

R-Truth made his comeback during the Survivor Series Premium Live Event and has been on a mission to join The Judgment Day. A lot of things happened between him and Damian Preist on RAW this week, but the two shared a moment after the show ended.

R-Truth has been actively involved in the ongoing Judgment Day storyline on Monday Night RAW, making efforts to insert himself into the angle somehow. This includes him paying the members their cut of R-Truth’s special Judgment Day-related merchandise.

During the main event of this week’s episode of Monday Night RAW, Damian Priest took on Drew McIntyre in a singles match. R-Truth ultimately cost Priest the match by unintentionally distracting the referee, allowing McIntyre to hit the Claymore kick on Priest for the win.

After Monday Night RAW went off the air, R-Truth came to Damian Priest and raised his hand. Priest would then signal to Truth to go to the back and he happily followed, even doing a heel click in the process.

In case you didn’t know, Damian Priest advocated for R-Truth to work with The Judgment Day in the first place. We will have to wait and see how R-Truth and The Judgment Day’s storyline progresses in the coming weeks, as fans are certainly invested in whatever Truth does.
