Robert Pattinson and Suki Waterhouse Welcome First Child

July 15, 2024
blog

Congratulations are in order for Robert Pattinson and Suki Waterhouse, as they have officially welcomed their first child together.

While the details of Waterhouse's birth and sex of the baby are still underwraps — the pair is relatively private about their relationship, after all — she and Pattinson subtly announced that their bundle of joy arrived in recent weeks via photo, where they were spotted by paparazzi pushing a stroller around while taking an afternoon walk around Los Angeles. The family of three was joined by Waterhouse's mother Elizabeth for the stroll.

The last time that Daisy Jones & The Six star was spotted was on February 24, where she was still several months pregnant — at the time, she and the Twilight actor were seen taking another walk around Los Angeles. Waterhouse's socials have been dormant since that week, with her last post on February 28 revealing that her forthcoming album is complete (her baby bump was still present in the images she shared).

Waterhouse formally announced her pregnancy back in November, while performing at the Corona Capital Festival in Mexico City. She took to the stage in a glittering dress that showed off her bump. "I thought I'd wear something sparkly today to distract you from something else I've got going on," the singer said to a cheering crowd. "I'm not sure if it's working."

This marks both Waterhouse and Pattinson's first child. The couple has been together for more than five years, with the pair first making their red carpet debut in December 2022 at the Dior’s Men’s Fall show in Giza, Egypt. The couple later went Met Gala official in May of 2023.

Pattinson and Waterhouse, 37 and 32 respectively, are currently engaged to be married. People revealed that they graduated from boyfriend and girlfriend to future spouses in December 2023, “They are engaged. They both want to be married. It’s important for them,” a source told the outlet, adding that Pattinson was "so ready" to become a father.
