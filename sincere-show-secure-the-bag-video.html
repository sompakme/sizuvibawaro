Sincere Show &quot;Secure The Bag&quot; Video

August 29, 2024
blog

If you're a hip-hop fan that grew up in the 90s, you should have the opening scene of Hype Williams' Nas and DMX-starring feature film Belly engraved in your memory. Chicago promoter turned rapper seems to be in that boat, recreating the iconic opening in his video for "Secure The Bag." It's a fittingly modern update, soundtracking the stylish homage with Sincere's very 2016 brand of street rap, his confident hook dripping in auto-tune.
