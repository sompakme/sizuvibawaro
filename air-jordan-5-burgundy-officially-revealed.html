Air Jordan 5 &quot;Burgundy&quot; Officially Revealed

July 17, 2024
blog

If you are a fan of the Air Jordan 5, then the last couple of years have been a whole lot of fun. We say this due to the fact there have been a lot of new releases for this shoe. Overall, this is mostly because the sneaker celebrated a 30th birthday in 2020. Consequently, Jumpman wanted to come out with as many retros as humanly possible. Additionally, they made sure to drop a lot of dope new colorways as well. In the end, it was a great stretch for the sneaker, and it seems like the hype is continuing.

Although Jumpman isn't treating the Air Jordan 5 like it is 2020 again, we are still seeing some solid teasers hit the market. Over these past couple of months, one shoe that people have been talking about is the Air Jordan 5 "Burgundy." This is a sneaker that first came out back in 2006. It was an amazing shoe back then, and it is still great now. Subsequently, it is set to come back out in 2023, and now, product shots are hitting the internet. These latest ones come from JD Sports.

"Burgundy" Air Jordan 5

Image via JD Sports

In the images above and below, you can clearly tell that this sneaker does a good job of living up to the OG. There are a lot of fans who were counting on Jumpman to deliver, and that is exactly what they did here. Firstly, the sneaker comes prepared in burgundy suede all throughout the upper. Secondly, more burgundy is found on the midsole, where a grey cement shark tooth is found. Additionally, we get a silver 3M tongue with the iconic burgundy Jumpman logo right in the middle. When you put it all together, you get a truly fantastic offering.

Overall, this shoe is set to be released on September 9th for a price of $225 USD. Hopefully, these live up to the lofty expectations placed upon them by the fans.
