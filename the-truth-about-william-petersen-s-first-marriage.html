The Truth About William Petersen's First Marriage

September 16, 2024

"CSI" star William Petersen is extremely private. The "CSI: Vegas" actor holds no social media accounts and keeps a very low profile. There is almost no information on Petersen's first wife, Joanne Brady. Cheat Sheet reported that the actor married Brady in 1974, and the couple's daughter Maite was born in 1975. Brady and Petersen divorced in 1981, and there is no public information on why the couple divorced. Petersen has never talked about Brady in any interviews, and there are no photographs of the couple. Just like her mother, there is very little about Maite. She has one credit on IMDb, playing a schoolgirl in the 1987 film her dad starred in, "Amazing Grace and Chuck."

Maite made Petersen a grandfather in 2003 when she welcomed her son Mazrik to the world. Maite's second son Indigo was born in August 2009, per SheKnows. It turns out that 2003 was a big year for Petersen! Entertainment Weekly reported in June 2003 the" CSI: Crime Scene Investigation" star wed his longtime girlfriend, Gina Cirone, in Italy. Like Brady, there is little public information about Petersen's second wife, Cirone. According to SheKnows, Cirone was a biology teacher, but no reputable sources with additional details about her.

While the actor is mum about his personal life, the "CSI" star will discuss Gil Grissom's marriage to Sara Sidle. Petersen told TVInsider, "Our attraction to each other was always based on the work."
