  • A Nigerian pastor recently advised his congregation on the need to take out money from their salary for leisure
  • In a video trending online, the pastor kicked against the habit of insisting on saving money and refusing to spoil oneself

A pastor's admonishment on the need to spend a part of one's salary on recreation has been trending online.

The pastor, Barnabas Alumogie, emphasised the importance of spending on food, furniture and other things that would make a person feel happy and fulfilled.

Pastor advises congregation to spoil themselves

In the video shared by @irishadassah on TikTok, the pastor advised his members to take a part of their salary and enjoy themselves.
