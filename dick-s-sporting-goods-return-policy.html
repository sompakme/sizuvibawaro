Dicks Sporting Goods Return Policy

August 15, 2024
blog

Dick’s Sporting Goods is a leading retailer of sporting goods, footwear, and apparel in the United States. The company was founded in 1948 by Richard ‘Dick’ Stack and has since grown to become one of the largest sporting retailers in the country.

Over the years, the chain has expanded rapidly, developing from a small regional retailer to one of the most prevalent sports stores in the country. Today, there are over 700 Dick’s Sporting Goods stores in the United States, serving millions of customers each year.

But what happens if you are unhappy with a purchase for whatever reason?

Let’s find out in my in-depth guide to the Dick’s Sporting Goods Return Policy.

What Should I Know Before Shopping at Dick’s Sporting Goods?

Dick’s Sporting Goods has a long history of providing customers with high-quality products, excellent customer service, and state-of-the-art tech at an affordable price.

The company is known for its exceptional customer service, offering knowledgeable and helpful staff, easy returns and exchanges, and a satisfaction guarantee on all products. Whether shopping in-store or online, you can count on Dick’s to provide the information and support you need to make the best choices for your sporting needs. 

An award-winning company…

Another key aspect of Dick’s Sporting Goods is its unique in-store experience offering a well-designed and well-lit shopping environment. The company has also invested in technology to enhance the shopping experience for its customers, including a user-friendly website, mobile app, and interactive displays in-store. 

The website and mobile app offer a range of unique features, allowing customers to access product reviews, view product recommendations, and the ability to track orders. Subsequently, Dick’s Sporting Goods has won several awards and recognitions, including the Sporting Goods Industry Award and the 2022 Retailer of the Year Award. 

A positive impact…

The company has also been recognized for its commitment to sustainability, winning the Corporate Social Responsibility Award from the Pittsburgh Business Times. Dick’s strives to reduce its environmental impact and promote sustainable practices, implementing various programs to minimize its carbon footprint and reduce waste.

These awards, recognitions, and initiatives highlight Dick’s commitment to providing high-quality products and customer service and its dedication to positively impacting its customer’s communities. 

But, What About the Dick’s Sporting Goods Return Policy?

Dick’s Sporting Goods has a flexible and customer-friendly returns and exchanges policy. It states that items purchased at Dick’s Sporting Goods can be returned within 60 days of purchase with a valid receipt or order confirmation. Items must be returned in an unused condition, in their original packaging, and with all tags and accessories included to ensure they are accepted.

In-store returns can be made at any Dick’s Sporting Goods location, and customers can also exchange items for a different size or color in-store. Online orders can be returned either by mail or in store, with a pre-printed return label included with the order, following the return policy guidelines. Just bear in mind that if an item is returned by mail, the customer is responsible for the return shipping costs.

Refunds are issued to the original form of payment, except for items purchased with a gift receipt, which will be refunded as merchandise credit.

Want to know what products cannot be returned to Dick’s Sporting Goods?

Most items purchased at Dick’s Sporting Goods are eligible for return or exchange; however, some may be subject to special return restrictions. Firearms, ammunition, opened software, gas-powered products, and other select items are not eligible for return. Similarly, special order items may have different return policies, so it’s important to check with a store associate before making a purchase.

Products and Services Offered at Dick’s Sporting Goods

One thing that sets Dick’s Sporting Goods apart from competing retailers is its wide range of products and services, available both in-store and online. So, let’s see what they have on offer.

Products

The company offers a comprehensive collection of sporting brands, exercise equipment, and exclusive products that are unavailable elsewhere, including a large selection of athletic clothing and footwear for men, women, and children, such as shirts, pants, jackets, and sports shoes. The company is also an official retailer of licensed sports merchandise, including jerseys, hats, and other fan gear for all your favorite teams.

The store also boasts an extensive catalog of sports and home gym equipment, ranging from golf clubs and basketball hoops, to treadmills, exercise bikes, and weight sets. And if you are a lover of the great outdoors, Dick’s has you covered, offering a variety of camping, hiking, and hunting gear, including tents, sleeping bags, fishing rods, and firearms.

Services

In addition to its extensive product offerings, Dick’s provides various services to enhance its customers’ shopping experience, including in-store pickup, product clinics, and repair services.

Select stores offer product clinics where customers can try out different gear, receive training and advice, and get hands-on experience with different products. This is a fantastic way for customers to get a feel for the products before making a purchase and learn more about the options available.

The store also has a trade-in program, where customers can trade in their used gear for credit toward new purchases at Dick’s stores. This is great for customers to get rid of old, unused equipment and upgrade to more advanced products at a discounted rate.

Dick’s Sporting Goods also provides repair services for bicycles and other equipment, making it convenient for customers to shop while their gear is being fixed.
