When you’re ready to step out of those high heels and pair your baggy jeans or low-rise denim with something more casual, you really can’t go wrong with a great pair of sneakers. A step up from basic white kicks, retro runners transition well for casual back-to-the-office looks for fall and beyond, all while looking to the past to add a bit of old-school playfulness to modern-day wardrobes.

Rihanna recently released her own take on the vintage shoe style with her Fenty x Puma Avanti VL sneakers, which quickly sold out (however, there are some available at StockX), and Veja has debuted a new ’90s-inspired silhouette, the V-90.

Related Stories

Meghan Markle has worn eco-conscious sneakers from Rothy’s and Veja, and classic athletic footwear brands like Adidas, New Balance (recently spotted on Taylor Swift) and Nike are regularly seen on stars including J. Lo, Gigi Hadid, Hailey Bieber, Naomi Watts, Shay Mitchell and Katie Holmes, to name just a handful.

If you’re looking to add vintage-inspired flair to your fall ensembles without crossing too far into the dad shoe trend or ugly sneaker territory, we’ve rounded up the best retro sneakers for women. You’ll find a slew of unisex trainers from the above go-to sportswear labels, as well as sustainable styles from Everlane, Madewell and others. See all of our top picks ahead.

1. Veja V-90 Sneakers

Hands-down one of the best eco-friendly sneaker brands, Veja is regularly seen on the feet of stars like Chloe Grace Moretz, Emily Ratajkowski and Emma Watson. The Certified B Corp. brand’s new V-90 sneakers are a nod to the ’90s and are made of 100 percent organic traced leather sourced from Uruguay, which Veja says is “known for its culture of animal husbandry and the quality of the leather.” The leather is tanned in Brazil using a process that requires less water, and it’s sourced from cattle that are not from deforested areas or exposed to antibiotics, pesticides, GMOs or chemical fertilizers. They’re finished in the label’s signature V appliqué on the sides and logo print on the back collar, and come in other colorways.
