&quot;Life is Hard&quot;: Nigerian Man Uses Multi-million Naira Lexus Car to Sell Bread, Video Causes Buzz

September 04, 2024
blog
  • A Nigerian man has been spotted selling loaves of bread from the boot of a beautiful Lexus car
  • In a video shared on TikTok, a man held two loaves of bread while speaking to some potential customers in the streets
  • The video generated mixed reactions as some said there was nothing wrong with it, while others blamed the harsh economy

Mixed reactions trailed the video of a Nigerian man who used a multi-million naira Lexus car to sell bread.

In a trending video on the TikTok handle of @d_evilone_sami, the man was spotted in the streets with loaves of bread in his hands.

The rest of the bread was loaded in the boot of the black Lexus car, which looked new and very clean.

He was talking to some people who appeared to be his potential customers.
