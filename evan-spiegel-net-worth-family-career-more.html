What Led to Spiegel’s Tech Stardom with Snapchat?

Snapchat first made waves at Stanford University when product design student Evan Spiegel imagined an app for instantaneous messaging, or “picaboo”, as its prototype is sometimes known. He later joined forces with Bobby Murphy and Reggie Brown – two fellow Stanford University students – and together launched Picaboo: this would eventually become Snapchat. Despite initial skepticism from users, its unique take on social media messaging by enabling users to send disappearing photos and videos has captured millions’ imagination.
