Drake Announces Toronto Raptors Starting Lineup

September 03, 2024
blog

On Wednesday night, the Toronto Raptors held their annual “Drake night” at the Air Canada Centre where they took on the Lebron James-led Cleveland Cavaliers.

Fans in attendance were treated with a commemorative T-shirt, headband, and chance to dance in a replica of the Hotline Bling video set, but that wasn’t all. Like he did last year, Drizzy also announced the night’s starting lineups, which included some extra fun jabs at the players too, like….“From Missouri, a 6’9” forward often mistaken for the R&B singer PartyNextDoor number 5 Damarre Carroll.”
