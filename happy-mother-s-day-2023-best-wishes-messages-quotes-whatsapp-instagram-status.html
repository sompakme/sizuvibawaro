Mother’s Day is an Auspicious day as it celebrates all the thankless contributions which mothers make every day. This Significant day is celebrated on the Second Sunday of the month of May in all the major Countries. The holiday is a way to recognize the contributions and sacrifices that mothers make for their families and society. The origins of Mother’s Day can be traced back to ancient civilizations such as the Greeks and Romans.

However, the modern celebration of Mother’s Day began in the early 20th century when Anna Jarvis, an American activist, campaigned for a national day to honour mothers. In 1914, the President of the United States made an announcement and signed a declaration to make the Second Sunday of the month of May to be celebrated as Mother’s Day in the USA.
