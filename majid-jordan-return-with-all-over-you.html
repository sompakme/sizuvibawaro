Majid Jordan Return With &quot;All Over You&quot;

July 24, 2024
blog

OVO’s Majid Jordan have been relatively quiet since they dropped off The Space Between album last year, but that doesn't mean they haven't been working. Having concentrated on touring and working on their next album, Majid & Jordan are now ready to show the world what they’ve been working on this year. Earlier this week, the duo informed fans that they were dropping off not one, but two new songs this Friday, and alas here they are.

Having already highlighted the song “Spirit,” here goes the second offering called “All Over You.” Available on iTunes, take a listen to the upbeat, self-produced dance cut and let us know what you think in the comments.

“When we release music it is about sharing what we love to do with people we love.I am so blessed to be alive. I do this for me as much as I do it for you. nothing changed :)” Jordan said a couple weeks ago. Enjoy!
