WWE NXT New Years Evil 2024 Preview: Confirmed Matches, Start Time and How to Watch

September 20, 2024
blog

WWE NXT New Year’s Evil will be taking place on January 2nd and there are quite a few matches for fans to look forward to. After all, the New Year’s Evil special is the first NXT show of 2024.

Tonight’s episode of WWE NXT will be taking place at the Capitol Wrestling Center in Orlando, FL, and will be airing live at 8 pm Eastern time slot on USA Network. Naturally, fans are excited for the show.

NXT Deadline’s Iron Survivors are set for their title shots, as Trick Williams targets NXT Champion Ilja Dragunov, and Blair Davenport aims to claim the Women’s belt from Lyra Valkyria.

Joaquin Wilde & Cruz Del Toro will join forces with a surprise LWO partner after Dragon Lee confirmed he couldn’t make it. They will face No Quarter Catch Crew in tag team action. Tiffany Stratton and Fallon Henley will compete in a “Servant or Ranch Hand” match, leading to one working for the other. In the Men’s Breakout Tournament finals, Riley Osborne aims to take down Oba Femi, while Roxanne Perez goes up against Arianna Grace.

WWE NXT New Year’s Evil Lineup (1/2/24):

  • WWE NXT Title: Ilja Dragunov (c) vs. Trick Williams
  • WWE NXT Women’s Title: Lyra Valkyria (c) vs. Blair Davenport
  • Men’s Breakout Tournament Final: Riley Osborne vs. Oba Femi
  • Joaquin Wilde, Cruz Del Toro and Mystery Partner vs. No Quarter Catch Club
  • Arianna Grace vs. Roxanne Perez
  • Ridge Holland will speak on hurting Ilja Dragunov

Ringside News will be providing live play-by-play coverage of tonight’s show over in our WWE NXT coverage hub. It promises to be a memorable episode for many reasons, so it remains to be seen how the episode will turn out.
