Where Are They Now Laguna Beach?

July 06, 2024
blog

MTV’s “Laguna Beach: The Real Orange County” was a cultural phenomenon when it first aired in 2004, introducing viewers to the sun-soaked drama of high school students in the affluent California city. The reality show ran for three seasons, following the lives of its cast members as they navigated friendships, relationships, and the brink of adulthood. But since the show ended in 2006, fans have often wondered what happened to the stars of this once must-watch reality TV staple. Let’s dive into the lives of the “Laguna Beach” alumni and discover where they are now.

Lauren Conrad

Lauren “LC” Conrad was the girl next door with big dreams, and she arguably became the most successful alum of “Laguna Beach.” After the show, she starred in her own spin-off, “The Hills,” which chronicled her life in Los Angeles as she pursued a career in fashion. Since then, Lauren has:

  • Launched her own fashion lines, LC Lauren Conrad and Paper Crown.
  • Written several books, including the best-selling “L.A. Candy” series.
  • Founded The Little Market, a nonprofit fair trade shop.
  • Married William Tell, with whom she has two children.

Stephen Colletti

Stephen Colletti, the heartthrob caught in a love triangle with LC and Kristin Cavallari, took his charm to the acting world. Post-“Laguna Beach,” Stephen’s endeavors include:

  • Starring on The CW’s “One Tree Hill” as Chase Adams.
  • Appearing in various TV shows and movies.
  • Co-hosting a podcast with fellow “One Tree Hill” alum James Lafferty.

Kristin Cavallari

Kristin Cavallari, known for her feisty personality and relationship with Stephen, continued her reality TV career with “The Hills” after Lauren Conrad’s departure. Since then, Kristin has:

  • Launched her own jewelry and lifestyle brand, Uncommon James.
  • Authored a New York Times bestseller, “Balancing in Heels.”
  • Starred in her own reality show, “Very Cavallari,” focusing on her business and personal life.
  • Divorced NFL player Jay Cutler, with whom she shares three children.

Lo Bosworth

Lo Bosworth was Lauren’s close friend and a secondary cast member who later joined “The Hills.” After reality TV, Lo has:

  • Graduated from culinary school.
  • Started a women’s wellness company, Love Wellness.
  • Become an advocate for mental health and wellness.

Talan Torriero

The smooth-talking Talan Torriero was known for his romantic escapades on “Laguna Beach.” After the show, Talan has:

  • Worked in digital marketing and advertising.
  • Married Danielle Torriero, and they have two children together.

Jason Wahler

Jason Wahler gained notoriety for his tumultuous relationship with Lauren Conrad on “The Hills.” His life after the show has been a journey of transformation:

  • Opened up about his struggles with addiction and recovery.
  • Become a public speaker and advocate for sobriety and recovery.
  • Married Ashley Slack, with whom he has a daughter.

Dieter Schmitz

Dieter Schmitz, a close friend of Stephen and LC, stayed out of the spotlight after “Laguna Beach.” He has since:

  • Built a career in hotel management.
  • Married Isabell Hiebl, and they have a child together.

Jessica Smith

Jessica Smith was often seen dealing with relationship drama on the show. Post-“Laguna Beach,” Jessica has:

  • Focused on her family life, marrying Michael Evans with whom she has four children.
  • Kept a relatively low profile compared to her fellow cast members.

Casey Reinhardt

Casey Reinhardt only appeared in the second season but made a memorable impact. After “Laguna Beach,” Casey:

  • Won the title of Miss California Teen USA.
  • Opened Casey’s Cupcakes, a successful bakery.
  • Married Sean Brown, with whom she has two children.

Alex Murrel

Alex Murrel was known for her singing aspirations and relationship drama. Since the show, Alex has:

  • Pursued a career in music and acting.
  • Married Kyle Mark Johnson, and they have two children.
  • Launched a lifestyle and wellness blog.

Morgan Olsen

Morgan Olsen, who struggled with college admissions on “Laguna Beach,” has since:

  • Graduated from Brigham Young University.
  • Worked in public relations and marketing.
  • Married Joel Smith, and they have children together.

Taylor Cole

Taylor Cole was another secondary character who has since:

  • Launched a career as a photographer.
  • Married Kevin Simshauser, with whom she shares children.

Cedric Channels

Cedric Channels, a friend of the main cast, has kept a low profile since the show. He has:

  • Reportedly worked in various jobs outside of the entertainment industry.

Christina Schuller

Christina Schuller, who aspired to be an actress and singer, has:

  • Become a certified fitness instructor.
  • Married Chad Sinclair, and they have children together.

FAQ Section

Did any of the “Laguna Beach” cast members continue in reality TV?

Yes, several cast members continued in reality TV. Lauren Conrad and Kristin Cavallari had their own spin-offs, “The Hills” and “Very Cavallari,” respectively. Jason Wahler also returned to “The Hills” reboot, “The Hills: New Beginnings.”

Have any of the “Laguna Beach” cast members written books?

Lauren Conrad and Kristin Cavallari have both written books. Lauren has penned several, including her “L.A. Candy” series, while Kristin wrote the bestseller “Balancing in Heels.”

What are some of the business ventures of the “Laguna Beach” cast?

Lauren Conrad has fashion lines, Kristin Cavallari has her brand Uncommon James, Lo Bosworth started Love Wellness, and Casey Reinhardt owns Casey’s Cupcakes.

Conclusion

The cast of “Laguna Beach” has come a long way since their days of high school drama on MTV. From fashion empires and best-selling books to advocating for wellness and sobriety, these former reality stars have carved out diverse paths for themselves. While some have stayed in the public eye, others have chosen a quieter life focused on family and careers outside of entertainment. Their journeys remind us that reality TV is just a starting point, and life beyond the beach can lead to unexpected and fulfilling destinations.
