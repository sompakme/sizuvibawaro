CJ Perry Drops Surprising Thanksgiving Revelation While Grocery Shopping

July 05, 2024
blog

Today is Thanksgiving in America, and people all over got their shopping in before the big day. It turns out that CJ Perry also did something she hadn’t accomplished for about ten years.

The former Lana has a lot of friends that she kept from WWE, and Liv Morgan is on top of that list. Although Liv Morgan may be out of action with an injury, she was still able to some shopping.

Liv Morgan and CJ Perry went to the grocery store, and that seemed like the perfect time for a video. Morgan whipped out her phone and she started recording. Then she made a very good point, one which Perry backed up as fact.

After Liv Morgan introduced the short video, she said that CJ Perry hadn’t been to a grocery store in about ten years. Then CJ Perry admitted that this is true. Obviously, she’s been famous for about that amount of time, so she must not have needed to go to the store, or she relied on delivery services.

“I haven’t been to a grocery store in a decade. I’m not even kidding.”

A lot of famous people stay out of the public, and apparently, CJ Perry was like that. She worked as Lana, a Ravishing Russian character for a while. Although she eventually dropped her accent, it would still be hard for her to hide in public.

We will have to see if CJ Perry and Liv Morgan go to any more stores to really break in Perry’s shopping experience. In the meantime, please stay tuned with us here at Ringside News.
