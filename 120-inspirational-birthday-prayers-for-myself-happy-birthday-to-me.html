120+ inspirational birthday prayers for myself: Happy Birthday to me

August 16, 2024
blog

Birthday prayers for myself are so unique that they make us feel closer to God. Whenever someone we know celebrates their birthday, we wish them well. But we often forget that we need to congratulate ourselves on our birthday, thanking God for the life that he gave us. Below is a list of birthday prayers for myself for your special day. Adetola Seedy, a coach, counselor, personal development trainer and the lead pastor at Peace Church Global, explains the importance of these prayers for one's life.

It's crucial to take time on your birthday and the birthdays of others you care about to thank the Lord for all that he has given you and look forward to all the good things that are still to come.

Why is birthday prayer for myself important?

Praying for yourself on your birthday is important because it is a way to thank God for the blessings you have received throughout the year. It is also an opportunity to set time aside to reflect on your life and to make plans for the next year.

Legit.ng journalist contacted Adetola Seedy, the lead pastor of Peace Church Global in Canada, to share his thoughts and opinions on the significance of these prayers for one's life:

I believe prayers should be said daily because it helps us get closer to God and gives us victory in our affairs. Based on this, I think it makes sense to pray even on our birthdays, because the bible instructs us to pray without ceasing (1Thessalonians 5:17). The general idea is that we are committing our new start to God and seeking His help to make it a successful journey. For people who are disciplined enough to always pray with fervency and desire, they always enjoy victory, regardless of the date on the calendar.

Pastor Seedy further explained that there is no wisdom in praying only on special days but every believer must take responsibility for his/her (spiritual) life and consistently pray over oneself.
