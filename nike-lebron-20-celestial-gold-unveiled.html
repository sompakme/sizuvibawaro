Nike LeBron 20 &quot;Celestial Gold&quot; Unveiled

September 10, 2024
blog

LeBron James and Nike snapped this year when they came through with the Nike LeBron 20. Overall, this has proven to be the best signature shoe of the entire year. To be honest, it has been quite a while since LeBron could be given such a designation. However, in year 20 of his illustrious NBA career, it was probably for the best that this happened. Either way, both casual fans and sneakerheads have appreciated these, which is certainly some amazing news for the good folks over at Nike.

Subsequently, we have been seeing some truly amazing colorways make their way to the market. The summer is almost here, which means a lot of you out there need shoes to hoop in at the outdoor courts. Additionally, this applies to kids too. Consequently, Nike has been cooking up some grade school colorways of the LeBron 20. Many of these colorways have been teased online over the last few weeks. Now, we have images of another Nike LeBron 20 for kids, this time called "Celestial Gold."

"Celestial Gold" Nike LeBron 20

Image via Finish Line

As many of you with kids already know, they love things that are colorful. When it comes to this "Celestial Gold" color scheme, we definitely get plenty of colors. Firstly, this shoe begins with a knit upper that has a gold base with some purple woven throughout. Secondly, the midsole is a light shade of blue with some purple on the bottom, and orange toward the back. Lastly, the Nike swoosh is iridescent, while the laces have a nice lavender finish to them. Overall, this makes for a great colorway that is perfect for kids.

At the time of writing this, the Nike LeBron 20 "Celestial Gold" does not yet have a release date. However, it will be dropping soon, so be sure to be on the lookout for it. If your kid needs shoes to hoop in this summer, these would be amazing. Let us know what you think of them, down in the comments section below. Additionally, stay tuned to HNHH for the latest news from the sneaker world.
