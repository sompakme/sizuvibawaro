    Is there anything that Kate Moss can't do? Between launching her wellness brand Cosmoss and continuing to warm the hearts of 90s supermodel-obsessives all across the globe, the 48-year-old is never idle.

    Just recently we were admiring her shimmery take on the current visible lingerie trend, worn last month to Diet Coke's 40th birthday celebration. But on closer inspection, it appears that her stunning ultra-plunge Bottega Veneta gown was not the only piece from that ensemble worthy of some major style envy.

    MORE: Celine’s Spring/Summer 2023 collection is an ode to Kate Moss’ 2000s era

    In a post shared on Instagram with Cosmoss' 51.5k followers, the new brand revealed details of its latest project. Exuding a goddess-like radiance in her statement sheer gown, courtesy of Bottega Veneta’s creative director Matthieu Blazy, Kate showed off three layered pendant necklaces, a striking triad of Blue Topaz, Prasiolite and Black Sapphire.

    Cosmoss, known for its "holistic approach to self-care and inner peace", announced that it is venturing into jewellery with a limited-edition collaboration with Amy Russell Taylor Jewellery and crystal healer Emma Lucy Knowles for their exclusive Oracle Dial necklace.

    View post on Instagram 

    "The Oracle Dial bridges the gap between the cosmos and your being. A triad of crystals to aid trust and understanding of the inner you and ‘knowing’ born from the Oracle within, allowing universal protection to flow all around you," the brand writes.

    "It was designed to be the ultimate key for opening your heart to the universe and connecting you to the cosmos…Sat within a design that continuously flows with every movement of the body, this piece aligns you to be the ultimate receptor for the purest vibrations that are in continuous motion around us."

    Suffice to say, where Kate Moss is involved, we're interested. And now getting our hands on Cosmoss' seriously stylish additions is all we can think about.
