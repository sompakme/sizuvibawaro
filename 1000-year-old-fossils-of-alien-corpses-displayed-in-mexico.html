1000-year-old Fossils Of 'Alien' Corpses Displayed In Mexico

August 20, 2024
blog

Two bodies, allegedly belonging to ‘alien’ beings were exhibited before politicians in a special session of the Mexican Congress.

A UFO scientist and journalist Jaime Maussen presented the fossiled bodies in glass cases. He says they were retrieved from Cusco in Peru and are said to be 1,000 years old.

“These specimens are not part of the evolution of our world. They were not recovered from a UFO scrap. They were found fossilized in a diatom moss mine,” Maussan said, testifying under oath.

Massan claims that these samples were analyzed by the National Autonomous University of Mexico and the scientists managed to get DNA samples by applying radiocarbon testing to determine their age. The fossils were found to be covered with a layer of sand.

The videos and pictures of these fossil bodies of alleged ‘aliens’ now turned sensational and viral.
