Recall that Atiku met with Peter Obi, the flagbearer of the Labour Party (LP) in the 2023 presidential election, on Monday, May 13. The photos of their meeting went viral. However, Atiku did not give details of his meeting with Obi.

The former VP wrote:

"It is never a dull moment whenever the former governor of Jigawa State, Alhaji Sule Lamido, comes calling. -AA"
