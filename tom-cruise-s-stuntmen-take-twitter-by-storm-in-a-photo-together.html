Tom Cruise's stuntmen take Twitter by storm in a photo together

September 14, 2024
blog

Almost all Hollywood stars have stunt doubles who perform their most dangerous stunts. The truth is that these actors have to be very similar physically to the stars, to the point where if you see them in the street or in a photo you might even have doubts about who the real movie star is.

This is what has happened with a photo of Tom Cruise and his doubles that has started to circulate on Twitter.

On the social networking website an image has emerged which shows the actor's stuntmen celebrating the end of filming of 'Mission: Impossible - Deadly Doom'.

Users have been left speechless by the resemblance between the three and have begun to speculate about a world populated by Cruise's clones.

A picture of Tom Cruise's clones invades Twitter

The comments have all been tongue-in-cheek and hilarious. Some confess that they didn't realize that none of the three members of the photograph was Tom Cruise and others doubt that it is really the actor who makes all those spectacular films and series.

"Tom Cruise stunt doubles MI:7 and MI:8 (the images are not AI)," wrote one internet user, accompanying the message with several images showing many of the same individuals as the 'Top Gun' star.

"Tom Cruise stunt doubles," said another, showing new images.

Both claim that these are real photo publications and that Artificial Intelligence has nothing to do with this phenomenon.

"They are real, they have no editing whatsoever," they stress.

It is worth noting that 'Mission: Impossible - Deadly Doom. Part One' will be released in Spain on 14 July, while the second half of the film will wait until 28 June 2024. In any case, the actor is already on everyone's lips after the conspiracy that says that his clones will dominate the world.
