Is there a Sierra free shipping code?

Sierra has free standard shipping for orders of over $89 with our shipping promo code. Standard shipping for orders of $89 costs $14.99 without a shipping code. If your order is less than $89, then standard shipping costs $9.99.  

Can I shop the Sierra sale?

You can shop the Sierra sales all year from the ‘Clearance’ page on their website. We’ve previously seen discounts of up to 85% off in these sales. Whether you’re shopping for sneakers or clothing, check the Clearance first for savings. Outside of the sales, our codes for Sierra can help you save on your favorite products too. 

Does Sierra have an email newsletter?

Sign up for the Sierra newsletter to be sent exclusive email discounts, information about the latest sales, early access to the latest product launches, and more. It's quick and easy to sign up, and it's also completely free. 

Can I earn rewards at Sierra?

Customers can earn rewards by applying for the TJX Rewards credit card. Successful applicants will receive 10% off their first in-store purchase, be able to earn 5% back when they shop online or instore, and will receive invites to exclusive private shopping events. 

Does Sierra accept returns?

Sierra has a 30 day returns policy from the date of purchase. If you made your purchase online, you have 40 days to return your order. All items returned should be in their original condition. This means they should be unworn, unwashed, and with their original tags still attached. Return shipping costs $8.99 and this will be deducted from the refund.
